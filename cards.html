💧 Water Purification

  • Boil: 1 min rolling (3 min above 6,500ft)
  • Bleach 8.25%: 8 drops/gal, wait 30 min
  • Iodine: 5 drops/qt clear, 10 drops cloudy
  • Filter turbid water first (cloth/coffee filter)
  • Safe: faint chlorine smell after treatment
  • Dehydration: dark urine, confusion, no tears

🏥 Wound Care

  • STOP BLEEDING: Direct pressure 10+ min, elevate
  • CLEAN: Irrigate with clean water under pressure
  • CLOSE: Butterfly strips or sutures if deep
  • COVER: Clean dressing, change daily
  • INFECTION (act <24hr): spreading redness, pus, red streaks, fever, warmth
  • Do NOT remove embedded objects
Seek medical care at first opportunity

🚑 START Triage

RED (Immediate): Life-threatening, treatable now

YELLOW (Delayed): Serious but stable — can wait hours

GREEN (Minor): Walking wounded — treat last

BLACK (Expectant): Unsurvivable or deceased

Steps: 1) Walking? → GREEN. 2) Not breathing after airway → BLACK. 3) RR>30 → RED. 4) No radial pulse → RED. 5) Can't follow commands → RED.

Treat most saveable lives first

🌿 Universal Edibility Test

  1. Identify — research first if possible
  2. Test one plant part at a time
  3. Smell — unpleasant odor: reject
  4. Skin test — rub on wrist, wait 15 min
  5. Lip test — touch to lips, wait 3 min
  6. Mouth — hold on tongue 15 min, spit
  7. Eat 1 tsp, wait 8 hours
  8. Eat small portion, wait 8 more hours

Never: milky sap · umbrella flowers · red berries

When in doubt, don't

🔥 Fire Starting

Triangle: Heat + Fuel + Oxygen

Tinder: dry grass, bark dust, cattail fluff, fatwood

Kindling: pencil-size dry twigs, split wood

Fuel: wrist-size dry hardwood

Lays: Teepee (quick heat) · Log cabin (long burn) · Star (fuel-efficient)

Natural spark catchers: char cloth, amadou fungus, birch bark

Prepare tinder before striking spark

🪞 Signaling

Ground signals (10ft letters, open ground):

V = Need help   X = Need medical help   → = Going this way

Signal mirror: Hold near eye · V-fingers at arm's length · Target in V · Tilt mirror so sun's reflection fills V · Sweep toward aircraft

Whistle: 3 blasts = distress. Repeat every 3 min.

Rule: 3 of anything = distress

🧭 Navigation Without Compass

Shadow stick: Mark tip → wait 15 min → mark again → line = E-W. Left foot on first mark = facing North.

North Star: Big Dipper outer bowl × 5 lengths = Polaris (North)

Watch (N. hemisphere): Hour hand → sun, bisect to 12 = South

Moss: North side of trees (verify with multiple trees)

Travel straight lines; mark your path

🪢 Essential Knots

  • Bowline: Fixed loop, won't slip — rescue, rigging
  • Clove hitch: Quick attach to post — stakes, lashing
  • Square knot: Join ends — bandages (not load-bearing)
  • Sheet bend: Join ropes of different thickness
  • Trucker's hitch: Mechanical advantage — load tie-down
  • Figure-8: Stopper knot — prevent rope slipping through
Practice until automatic

🦷 Dental Emergencies

  • TOOTHACHE: rinse warm salt water; clove oil on cotton; ibuprofen for pain/swelling
  • ABSCESS (swelling, fever): salt-water rinses; needs antibiotic (e.g. amoxicillin) — see dosing tool
  • KNOCKED-OUT TOOTH: hold by crown, not root; reinsert in socket or keep in milk/saliva; act <30 min
  • LOST FILLING: temporary fill with sugar-free gum or dental wax; keep clean
  • BROKEN TOOTH: rinse; cover sharp edge with wax; protect from cold/heat
⚠ Facial swelling spreading to eye/neck = emergency · See pdfs/where-there-is-no-dentist

⚡ Solar / Power Troubleshooting

  • Nothing charging: check fuses & breakers first; check polarity (+/−)
  • Low/no panel voltage: clean panels; clear shade; check for cracked cells & loose MC4 connectors
  • Voltage at panel, none at controller: broken wire or blown inline fuse between them
  • Battery won't hold charge: check water level (flooded); test resting voltage — 12.0V = ~25%, 12.7V = full
  • Inverter off/beeping: low battery cutoff or overload — shed loads, recharge
  • Order to check: connections → fuses → battery voltage → panel voltage → controller → inverter
Measure with a multimeter in DC volts · See Energy section
